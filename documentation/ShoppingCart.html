loadProduct(product) → {ShoppingCart}

Load a product into the shopping cart, and set the product quantity to 0. If the product already exists in cart, it will be overwritten. The opposite of eraseProduct.

Note this will establish a 'live' connection to the supplied product object, and any changes to the product (mainly its price) will be reflected in the shopping cart.
